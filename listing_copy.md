## INTAKE FORM QUESTIONS
(Delivered via thumbprinted.com/intake or localhost:8765 — self-hosted wizard)

These are behavioral probe questions. Every question asks for a specific incident.
Do NOT rephrase into self-report. The specificity of the question is what makes synthesis work.

Q1 — Describe a decision you made that you're genuinely proud of.
Q2 — Describe a decision you'd make differently.
Q3 — Tell me about something you built that represents how you think more honestly than your resume.
Q4 — Describe a situation where people disagreed with your read. What did you do?
Q5 — What problem do you keep coming back to because you can't leave it alone?
Q6 — Tell me about a time you walked into a situation that wasn't yours to fix and fixed it anyway.
Q7 — Describe a time you deferred to someone else's judgment and it was the right call.
Q8 — What do the people who work most closely with you know that others don't?
