What You Get

Not a personality test.
A portrait.

Personality tests put you in a box. A portrait describes you specifically — the through-line in your decisions, your signature moves, the tension that generates most of your best work, and the blind spot you probably already know about but haven't named.

AutoGraph reads your answers to 8 behavioral probe questions — your real decisions, from specific incidents — and synthesizes what it finds. The output is grounded in evidence, not self-report.

Sample Portrait (excerpt)
The Core Pattern
"He builds before he has permission, then makes the thing too useful to stop. This isn't recklessness — it's a deliberate strategy for moving in systems that require demonstrated value before granting authority."
The Tension
"He believes deeply in systems and hates being controlled by them. Most of his best work lives in that friction."
The Blind Spot
"He already knows what he's going to do. He's usually just building the evidence that makes it undeniable to everyone else — which means the real decision happened earlier than anyone thinks, including him."

The Process

How AutoGraph works.

1

Complete your Round 1 intake

8 behavioral probe questions. Not what you believe about yourself — what you actually did, in specific situations. Takes 20–30 minutes. No right answers.

2

Receive your portrait

Within 5 business days. Your core pattern, signature behaviors, tension, and blind spot — grounded in what you wrote, not in general models.

3

Round 2 intake [Deep & Full]

You receive 8 personalized follow-up questions written specifically from your Round 1 portrait — not generic prompts, but probes designed to confirm or complicate exactly what we found. Your Round 2 portrait goes sharper.

4

Portrait Navigator access [Full]

Chat with your portrait. Ask how your patterns apply to a negotiation, a hire, a conflict, a decision. The Navigator answers through the specific lens of your findings — not general advice.
